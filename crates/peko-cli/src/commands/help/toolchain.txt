peko toolchain <SUBCOMMAND>

Inspects and installs build toolchains.

Toolchains are recorded in versions.json at the Peko root and live under
Compiler/toolchains/<os>/<arch>. Each carries a toolchain.toml describing its
target triple, compile flags, includes, and the link step.

SUBCOMMANDS
    list                  load every installed toolchain's toolchain.toml and
                          report which parse and which fail

EXAMPLES
    peko toolchain list
