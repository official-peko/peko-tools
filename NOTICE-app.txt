GTK, WebKitGTK, and the Linux desktop stack
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Linux builds only. A Linux AppImage bundles the shared libraries the webview
needs so the app runs on hosts that do not have them. They are redistributed
UNMODIFIED and linked DYNAMICALLY; each retains its own license.

The bundled set includes, among others:

  WebKitGTK, JavaScriptCore      LGPL-2.1-or-later, BSD-2-Clause
  GTK 3, GDK, gdk-pixbuf         LGPL-2.1-or-later
  GLib, GObject, GIO, GModule    LGPL-2.1-or-later
  ATK, at-spi2                   LGPL-2.1-or-later
  Pango, HarfBuzz, fribidi       LGPL-2.1-or-later, MIT, Old MIT
  cairo, pixman                  LGPL-2.1-or-later or MPL-1.1
  GStreamer and its plugins      LGPL-2.1-or-later
  libsoup, libsecret, libgudev   LGPL-2.1-or-later
  GnuTLS, nettle, hogweed        LGPL-2.1-or-later
  libgcrypt, libgpg-error        LGPL-2.1-or-later
  ICU                            Unicode-3.0
  libxml2, libxslt, sqlite3      MIT, public domain
  freetype, libpng, libjpeg,     FTL or GPL-2.0, zlib-acknowledgement,
  libwebp, zlib, brotli, lcms2   BSD-3-Clause, zlib, MIT

The LGPL components are dynamically linked, so you may replace them with your
own modified versions: extract the AppImage, substitute the library under
`usr/lib`, and repack.

Complete corresponding source for these libraries is available from the
distribution they were taken from. To request the exact sources used to build
this bundle, email contact@pekoui.com.

NOTE FOR APP AUTHORS: if you modify any of these libraries and ship the result,
the LGPL requires you to make your modified source available. Shipping them
unmodified, as the Peko bundler does, requires only this notice.
